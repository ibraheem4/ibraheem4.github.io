I am a huge fan of Amazon Web Services and continue to be impressed by the rapid development of the product offering. Recently I found out that with Route 53, Amazon's DNS service, users can now purchase a new domain name or transfer the management of an existing domain name. Previously, I would register domains with Bluehost.

Domain Name Registration is a small but critical product upgrade that solidifies AWS as a one stop shop for web and mobile infrastructure. Technically, Amazon is a reseller of the registrar GANDI, but that's none of my business.
