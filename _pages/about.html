About

Hello, my name is Ibraheem.

I'm a Software Engineer, currently attending Dev Bootcamp in San Francisco, California.

Prior to enrolling in DBC, I worked as an Account Director at MdotM, a Mobile Advertising company.

I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin in 2008 with a degreen in Marketing and worked as an Investment Banking Analyst at Lehman Brothers immediately following graduation. It was the best of times, it was the worst of times.

College was amazing! I served as President of my fraternity, Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., and as President of the Jester East Residence Hall Council. I also participated in a student Exchange Programme at ESCP Europe in the heart of Paris, France. Fun fact: established in 1819, ESCP Europe is the oldest business school in the world.

I grew up in Houston, Texas and have 7 siblings; 4 older brothers, 2 younger sisters and 1 younger brother.
